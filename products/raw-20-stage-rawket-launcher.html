The RAW 20 Stage Rawket Launcher is a 20 pack of assorted pre-rolled cones in 7 different sizes. Like all RAW cones, these are made with unbleached, unrefined paper so you know exactly what you're smoking, every time. These cones come with built in paper filters and come conveniently packed in sturdy cardboard packaging with a few packing sticks included. So get the Raw Rawket Launcher today and support your favorite RYO brand.
The cones come in 7 different sizes

  • 2 X Supernatural "Foot Long"
  • 2 X Emperador
  • 2 X Piecemaker
  • 2 X 98 Special
  • 2 X Lean
  • 4 X King size
  • 6 X 1 1/4
